# NkwaBiz

**Empowering small and medium businesses to manage, monitor, and grow effortlessly.**

---

## Overview

**NkwaBiz** is an all-in-one **business management platform** built to help local and small businesses streamline their daily operations.  
With NkwaBiz, business owners can **track sales**, **manage inventory**, **receive stock alerts**, and **analyze performance** — all in one dashboard.

Our mission is simple:  
> To digitize traditional retail processes and empower business owners to make data-driven decisions with ease.

---

## Key Features

 **Product Management** — Add, edit, or archive products easily.  
 **Low Stock Alerts** — Get instant notifications when stock levels run low.  
 **Sales Tracking** — Record all sales and access detailed transaction history.  
 **Export to Excel** — Generate and download sales reports instantly.  
 **Product Archiving** — Keep your records clean by archiving inactive products.  
 **Sales History Analytics** — View historical trends and insights.  
 **Secure Authentication** — Powered by JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for safe access.  

---

## Why NkwaBiz?

Many small businesses in emerging markets still rely on **manual record keeping**.  
This leads to **errors, data loss, and poor decision-making**.  

**NkwaBiz** solves that problem by providing a simple, intuitive, and affordable digital platform — helping local entrepreneurs transition into the digital economy without complexity.

---

## Technology Stack

- **Backend Framework:** Flask (Python)  
- **Database:** MySQL  
- **Authentication:** JWT  
- **Environment:** Python Virtual Environment (`venv`)  
- **Data Export:** Excel (CSV, XLSX)  

---

## Market Opportunity

Ghana’s SME sector contributes **over 70% of GDP** and employs **about 80% of the workforce** — yet, less than **25%** use any form of digital business management system.  

**NkwaBiz** bridges that gap by targeting small retailers, distributors, and market vendors seeking simplicity and efficiency in managing their daily operations.

---

##  Business Model

NkwaBiz operates on a **freemium model**:
- **Free Tier:** Basic product and sales tracking.
- **Premium Tier (₵50/month)**:  
  - Low stock alerts  
  - Excel export  
  - Sales analytics  
  - Business reports
